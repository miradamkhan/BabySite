Privacy Policy
Last Updated: January 29, 2026

Nest ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how it is stored, and what choices you have when using the Nest mobile application ("App").

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect and store information you provide directly in the App, including:

- Child profile information (for example: name, birth date, sex, and optional gestational age)
- Tracking logs (sleep, feeding, diaper, pumping, medication, growth)
- Parent profile details (for example: parent name)
- Preferences and settings (for example: reminders, quiet hours, onboarding state)
- Subscription state needed to determine feature access

2. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

- Provide core tracking and history features
- Generate summaries, insights, and recommendations
- Power reminders and in-app personalization
- Enable premium feature access based on subscription status
- Improve app reliability and user experience

3. Data Storage

Most app data is stored locally on your device. This includes logs, profile information, and many settings.

Some subscription-related information is processed through third-party services (such as Apple and RevenueCat) to validate purchases and entitlements.

4. Payments and Subscriptions

Purchases are processed by Apple through your App Store account.
We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

We may receive subscription status metadata (for example: active entitlement state) from RevenueCat to unlock paid features.

5. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party providers to operate key functionality, including:

- Apple App Store (in-app purchases and subscription billing)
- RevenueCat (subscription management and entitlement status)

These providers process data under their own privacy policies.

6. Permissions

Depending on enabled features, the App may request device permissions, such as:

- Notifications (for reminders)
- Other platform-specific permissions needed for app functionality

You can manage permissions in your device settings.

7. Data Retention

Data you enter is retained to support app functionality until you remove it, reset local app data, or uninstall the App.

8. Your Choices and Rights

You can:

- Edit or delete logs inside the App
- Reset onboarding/local state from in-app settings (where available)
- Uninstall the App to remove local storage data from your device
- Manage/cancel subscriptions via your Apple ID settings

If you have privacy-related requests, contact us using the email below.

9. Children's Privacy

Nest is designed for caregivers and parents to track baby-related data. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under applicable legal age without appropriate consent from a parent or guardian.

10. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information processed by the App. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. International Use

If you use the App outside your country of residence, you understand that your information may be processed in jurisdictions where privacy laws may differ.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date and, where appropriate, provide additional notice in the App.
